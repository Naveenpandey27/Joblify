# 🚀 Joblify (v1)

**Joblify** is an AI-powered job application copilot that helps you apply smarter and faster by optimizing your resume, generating cover letters, and preparing for interviews.

---

## ✨ What Joblify Does

Joblify takes a job description as input and helps you:

* 🔧 **Optimize your resume** for that specific job (ATS-friendly suggestions)
* ✉️ **Generate personalized cover letters**
* 🎯 **Prepare interview questions & answers** based on the role

---

## ⚡ Features (v1)

* **JD-Based Resume Optimization**
  Get actionable suggestions to improve your resume for any job.

* **AI Cover Letter Generator**
  Generate tailored cover letters in seconds.

* **Interview Q&A Generator**
  Practice with role-specific interview questions.

* **Credit-Based Usage System**

  * 3 free credits (no signup)
  * 10 credits after signup
